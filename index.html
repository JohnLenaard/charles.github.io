A Cinderella ending for the finals of the Men’s Futsal event as Hagonoy Campus Kingfishers stunned everyone with a miraculous goal at the midway of the second half of their matchup against the mighty College of Information and Communications Technology (CICT) Firefox, 1-0, to top off the last day of the Bulacan State University’s Palarong Pampamantasan 2023, October 20.

After the drought by both teams, (Jericko) Racho, of the Kingfishers, ignited the crowd after playing excellent defense against Paminutan of CICT, resulting in a slim opportunity to attack the goal. Racho maximized that opportunity and made the most out of it as he delivered what ended up being the final nail of the coffin of Firefox as they laid down and got buried.

 
The CICT could not bounce back after their gallant and aggressive attempt to score a goal. Kingfishers just smartly wasted away the time and played strong defense as they held onto their precious lead. The match got heated, but it ultimately ended with sportsmanship and camaraderie. The Hagonoy Campus remained unbeaten all throughout the event, they did not let CICT stain their record.
 

“Unang una, salamat kay Lord. Kasi simula first game, hindi nya kami pinabayaan. All throughout the elimination, mula semis hanggang finals, hindi kami natalo. Ang keypoint sa pag kapanalo na ito ay ang pag tutulungan namin. Pagiging matatag, at kahit nauunahan kami sa bola, hindi kami sumusuko. Laging last minute yung mga goal namin,” according to Jericko.
 

The Hagonoy Campus performed with discipline and patience in their attacks. They are masterfully exchanging attacks against Firefox. The two teams both had an equal amount of opportunities to score a goal. Evenly matched up, it just all came down to who would have the most hunger to win. The two teams gave all of their best, making the matchup intense and competitive.
 
According to their captain ball, (Aaron) Maniacop, “sobrang gagaling nila (teammate), saka palagi naming inuuna si Lord. Bago kami lumaban, kailangan pray, kasi Siya yung mga nagbigay ng talent na ito. Muli nag papasalamat ako sa Futsal Men, Congrats! Champion tayo.”
“Matagal ko tong inintay, since 2019. Binuo namin tong Futsal (Men’s team), noong 2019 natalo kami, tapos 3-1, hanggang dumating ng grand finals, tapos hindi kami pinalad. Siguro hindi para sa amin yun.
 

 Pero ngayon, masasabi ko na para sa amin talaga. Deserve ng mga player namin yun, at     coach, maraming salamat sa Hagonoy (Campus),” he added.
 

Even though the Hagonoy Campus’ players did not have enough recovery as their match schedule was stacked up, playing game after game, it did not hinder them from still giving their “A” game.
 
“Walang akong masasabi kasi napakasuwerte ko sa mga bata ko. Napakababait nila, masisipag, masipag sila mag training, at kahit umagang umaga nag ttraining sila. Lagi silang ready lumaban,” as per Geloe Galang, the Kingfishers’ head coach.
“Hindi ko rin talaga expected (na mag 6-0 sila). Kasi malalakas din yung mga kalaban kagaya ng CICT, Bustos (Campus), Sarmiento (Campus). Lahat kasi malakas talaga kaya hindi ko ineexpect na mananalo dirediretso talaga, hanggang sa championship,” he added.
